Always at your services

From project exploration to design concepts to finishing touches, we handle every aspect of the home renovation process. With everything under one roof, we set an extremely high bar for quality and efficiency

Custom design + build

We listen, brainstorm, and discuss ideas from the start in order to maximise the potential of spaces and areas that are important to you. This step informs and educates you on what project ideas are feasible and how much money is required to complete them.

Architecture + engineering

With the assistance of our architect, we present the project in printed form, from ideas to drawn plans. We move on to engineering once the final draught is representative of your goals. And if building permits are required, we will handle them for you.

Interior + exterior design

This final stage is all about paying close attention to the smallest details. Our designer will guide you through the process of choosing finishes, colour schemes, window treatments, flooring, trim, and much more. It's easy to become overwhelmed, but we're here to make it less stressful and more enjoyable.

Project management

We're ready to begin construction once you've given your approval to all of the planning and design decisions. All trades, materials, and supplies, as well as site operations, are supplied and operated by us. Our primary goal is to complete the job on time and within budget.

We're experienced, established, and ready to get to work if you're ready to start a new home improvement project.

With forward-thinking design, experience-driven insight, and professional level craftsmanship, we've helped hundreds of Fraser Valley residents like you build better living spaces since 2005.
